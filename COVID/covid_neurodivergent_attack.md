## PART 1: THE DIRECT GENETIC CONNECTIONS

### 1.1 ACE2 Receptor & Autism Genetic Overlap

**ACE2 = SARS-CoV-2 Entry Point**

Genetic variants of angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE) are linked to autism. This is the SAME enzyme system that COVID-19 uses to enter cells.

**EMx Analysis:**

**ACE/ACE2 System = O₆ (Normalization) Controller:**
- ACE2 regulates angiotensin II levels
- Angiotensin II affects inflammation, immune response, stress response
- In neurotypical: ACE2 maintains homeostatic balance
- **In autism: ACE gene variants alter O₆ efficiency**

**What This Means:**
- Autism genetics ALREADY have altered ACE system
- COVID-19 binds to ACE2 → **disrupts already-compromised O₆ pathway**
- Result: Double hit to normalization capacity

**Specific Variants:**
Three polymorphisms of ACE (I/D, rs4343, and rs4291) are associated with autism

- **I/D polymorphism:** Insertion/deletion in intron 16
  - DD genotype: Higher ACE activity (more aggressive O₆ attempts)
  - II genotype: Lower ACE activity (weaker O₆ baseline)
  - **Both variants vulnerable** - system already stressed

- **EMx Prediction:** Individuals with autism ACE variants will show:
  - ✓ Higher ACE2 expression (attempting to compensate)
  - ✓ More severe COVID symptoms (virus exploits high ACE2)
  - ✓ Longer recovery (O₆ system overwhelmed)
